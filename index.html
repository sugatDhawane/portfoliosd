About Me

Hey there! I'm Sugat, a Lead Frontend Engineer with a focus on building high-performance, scalable web applications. With over 15 years in software development, I've spent the last decade-plus mastering the frontend, specializing in modern frameworks and complex architectures.

Lately, I've been deep in the financial services space. At PNC Bank, I led a transition to Angular 18 and implemented Micro-frontends to streamline loan processing workflows. Before that, I spent nearly six years at Charles Schwab, where I worked across multiple projects to build accessible, reusable UI component libraries using Stencil.js and Angular.

I enjoy the challenge of turning complex technical requirements into seamless user experiences, whether that involves mentoring a team, optimizing CI/CD pipelines, or architecting for long-term scalability.

I'm also an advocate for modern workflows; I leverage AI tools like Cursor and GitHub Copilot to handle repetitive tasks, allowing me to focus more on high-level system design and performance.

I am a proud MCA (Master in Computer Application) graduate from Pune University and am currently available to join a new team immediately.
